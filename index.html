Welcome

Data Science / Engineering / Infra

My name is Chris, I'm predominantly a Data Scientist by trade (since 2017) but have worked in a variety of tech based roles. My current role is Head of Data Science at Shift in London.

I'm technically minded, but also possess the soft skills required to work with non-technical stakeholders and develop talent.

In the last few years I have taken a particular focus on pricing, and how data-driven pricing done properly can have revolutionary effects for businesses. Other focusses include vehicle routing and ChatGPT related projects.

Please take the time to explore the site, which includes all of my relevant skills and experience. You can get in touch via any of the social media icons at the top of this page.
