Welcome

Leader / Data Scientist / ML Engineer

My name is Chris, I'm predominantly a Data Scientist by trade but have worked in a variety of tech based roles. My current role is Head of Data Science at Shift in London. Please take the time to explore the site, which includes all of my relevant skills and experience. You can get in touch via any of the social media icons at the top of this page.

Specialisations

Leadership

I currently lead the data science strategy at Shift, which involves working directly with the C-suite as well as mentoring junior data scientists. I am also one of the lecturers at Nuclio Digital School, which runs a number of data science courses all over the world.

Pricing

I have led a number of projects around data-driven pricing, one of the most difficult yet exciting fields I have worked in so far. The most notable of these projects resulted in a 20% uplift in margin.

Customer Behaviour

I have spent a large part of my career using data to predict customer behaviour, focussed primarily around customer retention, marketing spend optimisation and pricing.

Infrastructure

Skilled in setting up the infrastructure required to run machine learning models in the cloud using Amazon Web Services. Currently managing the data science tech stack at Shift, and previously drove the development of a machine learning platform at Glovo.
