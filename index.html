Data Science / Engineering / Infrastructure

My name is Chris, I'm predominantly a Data Scientist by trade but have worked in a variety of tech based roles. My current role is Head of Data Science at Shift in London.
I'm technically minded, but also possess the soft skills required to work with non-technical stakeholders and develop talent. Being British I also don't mind conversing over a beer or two, especially if there's football involved.
Please take the time to explore the site, which includes all of my relevant skills and experience. You can get in touch via any of the social media icons at the top of this page.

Specialisations

Leadership

I currently lead the data science strategy at Shift, which involves working directly with the C-suite as well as mentoring junior data scientists. I am also one of the lecturers at Nuclio Digital School, which runs a number of data science courses all over the world.

Pricing

I have led a number of projects around data-driven pricing, one of the most difficult yet exciting fields I have worked in so far. The most notable of these projects resulted in a 20% uplift in margin.

Customer Behaviour

Multi-Disciplined
