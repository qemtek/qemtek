  • Development of a data-driven price elasticity model to help increase prices without affecting volumes

  • Extensive work into customer segmentation. The aim was to improve targetted marketting by identifying who our key customer groups were and creating custom marketing campaigns for these groups

  • Customer lifetime value modelling, used to determine the level of discount to give to customers in promotions

  • Sales forcasting at a store level, product level and overall business level. Used to set reasonable targets and highlight areas of the business in need of improvement

  • Key Technologies: R, Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau, Segmentation, Forecasting
